I am Ayn Fritzi Pachoco. I am an English Filipino translator, writer, and blogger. I was born in Antique, Philippines but grew up learning mostly Tagalog and English languages. My mother tongue is Tagalog; my second language is English.



I graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of the Philippines the Visayas with a major in Psychology and minors in Literature and History. I currently work as a government employee full time and have worked part-time as a content writer for blogs and ebooks for US and UK companies and as a freelance translator for various companies that need translation services between English into Tagalog or Tagalog into English.
